## What Scale AI is selling
Scale AI sells AI data infrastructure and evaluation platforms. In simple terms, they sell the foundation that makes AI models accurate, reliable, and usable in real-world production. They do not sell AI models themselves. They sell:
- High-quality training data
- Evaluation & safety systems
- Platforms to build, test, and operate AI applications at scale
## Capabilities / Offerings
#### Data Engine
- Data labeling & annotation (text, image, video, audio)
- RLHF (Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback)
- Synthetic & generated data
- Human-in-the-loop workflows for high-accuracy datasets
#### Generative AI Platform
- Build and deploy GenAI applications and AI agents
- Model evaluation & benchmarking
- Prompt management, feedback loops, monitoring
- Enterprise governance, security, and compliance
#### Safety, Evaluations & Alignment (SEAL)
- AI safety testing, red-teaming, bias detection
- Model alignment and trust assessment
- Creation of benchmarks like SciPredict to test real-world reasoning
#### Enterprise & Government AI Solutions
-  Secure AI pipelines for defense, healthcare, finance, and public sector
- Support for regulated, high-risk AI deployments
## What’s unique about Scale AI
- Data-first approach
- Scale treats data quality as the core product, not the model itself.
- Human + automation at scale
- Combines automation with trained human experts to achieve accuracy that pure automation cannot.
- Leader in AI evaluation & safety
- Creates industry benchmarks (e.g., SciPredict) instead of relying only on existing metrics.
- Trusted in high-stakes domains
- Used by governments, defense agencies, healthcare, and enterprises where errors are costly.
- End-to-end AI lifecycle coverage
- Supports everything from data creation → training → evaluation → deployment → monitoring.
