### **Assumptions**

1. **Content & Branding**
    - Swank will provide the branding guide.
    - Swank will provide access to current analytics, user behavior data, and current website data export.
2. **CMS & Hosting**
    - A new CMS will replace Umbraco; final selection will be made during the Discovery phase.
    - Swank is moving away from Umbraco due to flexibility and support issues.
    - Prefers a scalable, user-friendly CMS with modular content blocks, SEO controls, and role-based permissions.
    - Open to SaaS, headless, or open-source CMS recommendations.
3. **User Segmentation**
    - Website will differentiate between current customers, prospects, and students.
    - Students will be redirected to contact their school, not enter the sales funnel.
    - Primary personas: Librarians, IT/EdTech, Principals, Superintendents.
4. **SEO & Analytics**    
    - GA4, Google Tag Manager, and Microsoft Clarity will be used for analytics and heatmaps.
    - SEO strategy includes pre-launch audits, keyword mapping, and post-launch monitoring during support period only.
5. **Accessibility**    
    - ADA/WCAG 2.1 Level AAA compliance is a must.
    - Accessibility testing will include both automated and manual methods.
6. **Discovery Phase**    
    - Swank is open to a paid Discovery/Strategy phase and expects it to be collaborative and compensated.

---
### **Dependencies**

1. **Client Inputs**    
    - Timely access to stakeholders for interviews and feedback.
    - Approval of sitemap, wireframes, and design mockups.
    - Access to current content repositories.
2. **Third-Party Tools**    
    - Integration with Microsoft Clarity, Semrush, Tag Manager, and Google Analytics.
    - CRM integration with Dynamics 365. License will be provided by Swank.
3. **Content Migration**    
    - Swank to confirm which content is to be migrated, sunset, or rewritten.
    - Metadata tagging and structured data setup depend on content clarity.
4. **Training & Handover**    
    - Swank team availability for admin training and documentation review.

---
### **Out-of-Scope Items**
1. **Post-Launch Marketing**    
    - Ongoing content creation, media buying, and retargeting campaigns are explicitly out of scope.
2. **Student-Facing Features**    
    - No direct engagement or features for students beyond redirection.
3. **Legacy System Support**    
    - No support for maintaining or updating the existing Umbraco CMS.
4. **Ongoing Support**    
    - Optional post-launch support (ticketing/helpdesk) is not part of the main proposal.
5. **Custom Video Streaming Infrastructure**    
    - While educator tools and search/filter are included, building or hosting a full video streaming backend is not mentioned.
6. **Login/Portal**    
    - No authenticated experience or customer dashboard is required initially beyond CMS and analytics.
7. **Multi-language Support**    
    - Open to discussion but not required at launch.
8. **Post-Launch Optimization**    
    - CRO experiments and dashboards are optional and not part of the base scope.
